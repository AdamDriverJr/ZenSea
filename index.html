About us
On our Zen Sea page you will find a lot of useful information about the seas, oceans, aquatic animals and organizations that help to preserve all of it. There, you can take a look at various aquatic animals, admire their unique appearance and learn more about them - and you won't even need diving equipment for it. For those who are more interested in unique places which dot our beautiful oceans, you can just check our wonderful map. And if you’re like us are indifferent to ocean conservation, you can find out about organizations on our site, which are involved in that. Here you will find all the necessary contacts and all the detailed information on how and with what you can contribute to this noble goal.
